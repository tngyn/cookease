Welcome to Cook Ease!

This is our class project for Intro to Human-Computer Interaction Design led by Professor Scott Klemmer at UCSD. Please feel free to try our app out!

With Cook Ease, you can add ingredients into your inventory and find recipes you can make based off your current inventory.

It's for anyone who wants to spend less time looking around the kitchen for what they have and spend more time cooking.

It's for anyone who wants to try cooking with new ingredients.

Really though it's for everyone!

It's quite easy! Add ingredients from the search box in the inventory page.

Then look up recipes in the meal categories page.
