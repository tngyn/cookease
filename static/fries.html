Salt and Pepper Skillet Fries

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 3 large potatoes, sliced lengthwise into 1/2 inch circles and cut into 1/2 inch strips
  • 1/4 teaspoon coarse sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cracked black pepper

Directions

Step 1:  Heat olive oil and butter in a skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir potato strips in the hot oil-butter until browned on all sides, 20 to 25 minutes. Transfer fries to a paper towel-lined plate; season with salt and pepper.
